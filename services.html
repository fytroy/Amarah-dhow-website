Our Services & Activities

Beyond charters, we offer a range of unique sailing experiences.

Sailing Lessons & Courses

From beginner introductions to advanced navigation, our certified instructors offer private and group lessons. Earn your sailing certifications with us and gain confidence on the water!

  • Introduction to Sailing (1-Day Experience)
  • Basic Keelboat Certification (3-Day Course)
  • Advanced Coastal Cruising & Navigation
  • Private Tutoring & Refresher Courses

Bareboat & Skippered Charters

Choose your adventure! Rent a yacht for self-guided explorations (bareboat) if you're certified and experienced, or opt for a worry-free skippered charter with our expert captain at the helm.

Event Hosting & Corporate Retreats

Host your next special event onboard! Our yachts are perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, intimate weddings, team-building retreats, or unique corporate gatherings. We can customize catering and activities.

Regattas & Race Participation

Join us for the thrill of competition! Participate in exciting local regatta events or charter a boat to compete in sailing races. Learn race tactics, improve your skills, and experience the adrenaline of competitive sailing.

Fishing & Diving Expeditions

Combine your love for sailing with exhilarating offshore fishing or captivating diving adventures. Our crew can guide you to the best spots, providing equipment and expertise for an unforgettable marine experience.
