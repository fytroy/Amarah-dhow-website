Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Typically, our charters include the use of the vessel, safety equipment, and a professional captain (for skippered charters). Provisions, fuel, and marina fees are often extra, but this varies by package and duration of the charter. Please check your specific booking details for inclusions.

No prior experience is necessary for our skippered charters or sailing lessons. Our experienced captains handle all navigation and sailing. For bareboat charters (without our captain), you will need to demonstrate adequate sailing qualifications and experience.

We recommend bringing sunscreen, a hat, sunglasses, comfortable non-marking shoes, a light jacket (even on warm days), swimwear, and a camera. We'll provide safety equipment and fresh water. A detailed packing list will be sent with your booking confirmation.

Meal inclusions vary by charter type and duration. Day trips often include light snacks and non-alcoholic beverages. Multi-day and week-long charters may have optional catering packages available, or you can bring your own provisions. Please discuss your preferences when booking.
