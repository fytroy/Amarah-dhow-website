Our Philosophy

At Amarah dhow, our philosophy is simple: provide exceptional, safe, and memorable sailing adventures. We believe in connecting people with the beauty of the ocean while prioritizing comfort and sustainability.Proudly sailing the Swahili Coast.

Founded in 2025, we've grown from a single passion project into a premier charter service, known for our personalized itineraries and dedicated crew.
