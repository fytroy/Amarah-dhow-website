Popular Sailing Routes

The Emerald Coast Islands

A 5-day adventure exploring pristine beaches and vibrant marine life across 3 beautiful islands. Perfect for snorkeling and relaxation.

Sunset Bay Serenity

Sample Trip Itineraries

3-Day Coastal Discovery Cruise

Day 1: Depart Marina, sail to Coral Cove for snorkeling and swimming. Enjoy a fresh seafood lunch on board. Anchor overnight under the stars.

Day 2: Explore Hidden Beach, try paddleboarding or kayaking. Discover local flora and fauna on a guided shore walk. Sunset dinner on board with panoramic views.

Day 3: Leisurely sail back to Marina, enjoying a farewell brunch. Opportunity for a final swim before disembarking.

7-Day Archipelago Expedition

This comprehensive week-long journey delves deep into the Archipelago. It involves island hopping, cultural visits to local fishing villages, hiking to scenic viewpoints, and ample time for both adventurous sailing and tranquil relaxation in secluded bays. Custom stops and activities available.
